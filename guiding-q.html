Guiding
Questions

↓

Q1.

  • Where did I first encounter this particular cultural material?
  • Is there a strong reason why I chose to borrow from this particular culture?

Q2.

  • What is the goal of my project? Why do I need this particular cultural material?
  • Do I have enough resources on this culture? If not, how are you going to obtain them?

Q3.

  • Can I explain why using this particular material may cause dispute in some cultures?
  • Have I found examples that utilize this particular cultural material I am going to use?

Q4.

  • Non-credible sources are social networks, blogs, materials published over 10 years ago, and articles without citations.
  • Credible sources are academic databases, websites registered by government and educational institutions, and research articles with proper citations.
